About Us

Music Events is one of India’s leading COMMUNICATION & ENTERTAINMENT Company, integrating strategy, creativity and technology to provide unique experiences.

Music Events started as a dream and now has become a phenomenon in the event industry. They have tirelessly worked towards making Music Events, India’s leading communication and Entertainment Company and continue to do the same till date. Headquartered in Mumbai, Music Events has offices in Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Ahmedabad and Vizag in India, with international liaison offices and associates across the world.

Music Events, with a depth of expertise in experiential marketing, boasts of having in its client portfolio more than 500 leading MNCs and corporate houses including Microsoft, IBM, Zee TV, Bacardi, Audi, Standard Chartered, Wipro, Redbull, Adidas and many more. Music Events’s in-house brands include IIFA, GiMA, StarGuild Awards and Kingdom of Dreams. Music Events has successfully conceptualized, organized and executed large scale events such as the Opening and Closing ceremonies at the 19th Commonwealth Games, 50 years of Indian Independence Day Celebrations and Opening Ceremony at the Hannover Messe (2015).

Music Events is in the business of creating experiences and our staunch belief is that an event may be forgotten, but an experience will last a lifetime. Music Events, a pioneer in the events, exhibitions, activations, television, PR & digital space has been instrumental in shaping the event management industry in India.
